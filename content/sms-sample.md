---
title: "SMS Sample Messages"
---

Below are examples of the transactional SMS messages you may receive from Peoria IT when you opt in to repair status notifications.

## Repair Received

> Peoria IT: We've received your device for repair. We'll text you updates as we go. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg & data rates may apply.

## Diagnosis Complete

> Peoria IT: Diagnosis is complete for your repair. Please call us at (309) 839-2184 to discuss the results and next steps.

## Repair In Progress

> Peoria IT: Good news — your repair is now in progress. We'll notify you when it's ready for pickup.

## Repair Complete

> Peoria IT: Your device is repaired and ready for pickup at 4530 N Brandywine Dr. Thank you for choosing Peoria IT!

---

**Message frequency:** You'll receive messages only when your repair status changes (typically 2–5 per repair).

**Opt-out:** Reply **STOP** at any time to stop receiving messages.

**Help:** Reply **HELP** or call (309) 839-2184.

Message and data rates may apply.
